Recipe: Oma Soup

Oma used to make this soup for me growing up when ever I was sick. I remember staying home from school, watching Recess and Hey Arnold and eating this soup from the couch.

It uses basic ingredients and can be customized however you want. Which is part of why I love it so much. Oma always made it with carrots, which is why I included it here. But you can use any shreddable vegetable really.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal
  • Carrot
  • Broth
  • Egg
  • Seasoning (salt, pepper, an herb like parsley or something)

You'll need some sort of fat to toast the oatmeal. I use butter.

Directions

  1. Toast oats in a pan with fat
  2. Shred and aadd carrot to oats
  3. Add broth
  4. Whisk in egg
  5. Season to taste

So now you know what to eat the next time you or a loved one is sick. It's really very comforting and nourishing.
