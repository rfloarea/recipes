Recipe: Mum's Beef and Noodles

This meal is best enjoyed after a long day of work and little worry to using the "best" ingredients.

It's all about serving a simple meal that provides simple comforts.

Ingredients

  • Elbow noodles
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Large can of peeled tomatoes

Directions

  1. Chop and saute onion
  2. Saute beef in onion
  3. Add can of tomatoes
  4. Cook noodles
  5. Combine noodles and beef mixture

That's it! That's the World Famous Mum's Beef and Noodles.
